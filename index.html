Random Number Game

Who are you?


Rules:
1. Select your name.
2. Start this game with "Start Game" button.
You will get two random number. If the sum of two numbers are less then 21 then you will get a chance to try with random "New Number" button.
3. If the sum of all numbers are 21 then you will earn $20
4. If total sum grater then 21 till 29 then deduct $50
6. "Game Over" when sum 30 or grater.
5. Please refresh link to start new game.
6. *****Happy Gaming*****
